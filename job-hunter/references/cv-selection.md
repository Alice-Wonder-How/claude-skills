# CV Selection Guide

Alice has 3 CVs. Pick one per application.

## CMO / VP Marketing CV
Use when:
- Title is CMO, VP Marketing, Head of Marketing at a larger (50+) company
- Role owns full marketing org, budget, team hiring
- Executive-level, board-adjacent

Don't use for:
- Scrappy early-stage startups (too executive)
- Single-function PMM or Growth roles

## Head of Marketing / PMM / Senior PMM CV
Use when:
- Product marketing focus: positioning, messaging, launches, customer research
- Technical product that needs translation to a category
- Role mentions ICP interviews, competitive positioning, narrative
- B2B SaaS, API-first, infra, dev tools

## Growth / GTM Lead / Head of Growth CV
Use when:
- Growth, GTM, user acquisition, funnel, experimentation focus
- Scaling startup (Seed to Series B)
- Role mentions systems, loops, channels, CAC/LTV
- Default for: Nansen-type vibe marketer, scrappy growth leads

## Tie-breakers
- If role says "build the marketing function from scratch" at a small startup → Growth/GTM
- If role says "own positioning for technical product" → PMM
- If role says "lead 10+ person team, own brand and demand gen" → CMO/VP

State the pick in one sentence and why.
